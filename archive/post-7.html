Coming out as Transgender.

Content

Hello everyone, I'm officially coming out as Transgender. It may be a surprise for some but I feel like I need to address some potential questions.

When did you realize that you were Transgender?

I think it started back in elementary when I was visiting a hotel, I saw people wearing swimsuits (I was told to look away), but I felt like I wanted to wear them, for some reason.

Then a few months later during the final examination, I was asked to fill out a form about if I'm fine with god making me a boy or something, I wrote that I was okay with it, but for some reason, I feel like I was lying to myself, I ignored that feeling.

In my teenaged years, I honestly didn't really felt comfortable about my body, I managed to curb the feeling, but sometimes I really wished I had a feminine one instead, I never told my parents about this and I felt like they would banish me from the house.

All of this caused a ton of Dysphoria and I feel like I need to get it off my chest.

Will you do HRT/Sex Change Surgery?

I honestly do not know, I do not want to burden my parents even more and I'm really afraid of the disastrous permanent consequences it could have.

Are you really sure you're Transgender?

I can never be sure. I feel like I'm Non-binary leaning towards female, I can never be 100% sure. but based on what I perceive myself, I think I'm Transgender.

Postscript

Well, that does it for this very personal article, my name is now Dania Rifki, my pronouns are She/Her. and goodbye.
